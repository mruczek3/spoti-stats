### Option 2: Setup for Real Spotify Data (5 minutes)
```
1. Go to http://localhost:5173/callback
   (This shows your current redirect URI)
   
2. Visit https://developer.spotify.com/dashboard
   
3. Select your app → Edit Settings
   
4. Add Redirect URI:
   http://localhost:5173/callback
   
5. Click "Save"
   
6. Back to http://localhost:5173
   
7. Click "🎧 Login with Spotify"
   
8. Grant permissions and you're set!
```
