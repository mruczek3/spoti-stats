# 🎵 Spotify Stats Dashboard

Discover your music taste in stunning detail. Spotify Stats analyzes your listening history and creates beautiful visualizations of your personal music personality.

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT) ![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Live-brightgreen)

**[🎧 Launch Dashboard](https://mruczek3.github.io/spotify-stats/)**

---

## ✨ What You'll Discover

See your music in 8 stunning dashboards:

🏠 **Dashboard** - Your personalized music profile  
🎵 **Top Tracks** - Your favorite songs ranked  
🎤 **Artists** - Your most-loved artists  
🎨 **Profile** - Your unique listening personality  
⏰ **Listening Patterns** - When you listen most  
🌍 **Genres** - Your musical universe  
😊 **Mood Timeline** - How your taste evolves  
📊 **Report Card** - Shareable listening stats

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Option 1: Connect with Spotify (Recommended)
1. Visit the [Spotify Stats Dashboard](https://mruczek3.github.io/spotify-stats/)
2. Click **"🎧 Connect with Spotify"**
3. Log in to your Spotify account
4. Grant permission - See your stats instantly!

### Option 2: Try Demo Mode
1. Visit the [Spotify Stats Dashboard](https://mruczek3.github.io/spotify-stats/)
2. Click **"👀 View Demo"**
3. Explore with sample data (no Spotify account needed)

## 🎨 Design

- Spotify Green (#1DB954) accents

## 🔐 Privacy & Security

✅ **Your data stays yours**
- All processing happens in your browser
- No data sent to external servers
- Works completely offline (demo mode)
- PKCE OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
